Cryptos vs. Indexes

Understanding their Relation to Predict the Future

Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies are digital currencies that are secured by cryptography. They are becoming increasingly popular and valuable, and have sometimes been referred to as “the currency of the future”

 

Indexes

Indexes are used to track the performance of a group of assets or securities.

 

Project Scope

For our project, we selected cryptocurrencies at 3 price points:

 

  • Bitcoin (High, ~$50,000 USD)
  • Ethereum (Mid, ~$2,500 USD)
  • Litecoin (Low, ~$250 USD)

 

And compared them to 3 indexes:

  • S&P 500 Index
  • U.S. Dollar
  • Gold

  • April 2, 2018 – April 2, 2021
