Summary: Investment Instruments

Investing in the stock market is a very good way to create wealth and insure that you and your family are provided for. There are some general rules for investing that are always good to remember. But there are risks and rewards for investing. Different investors have different levels of tolerance for that risk. Generally, there are investors and traders. An investor generally take a longer term view for their investments. An investor will buy a stock based on the fundamental value of the company or commodity. They tend to buy a stock and hold onto it for a longer term. A trader on the other hand will buy a stock based on technical analysis. They could make tend multiple trades in a day. This type of analysis looks at the trading trends for the stock or commodity. Each of these methods has risks and rewards associated with it and easch trading method has greater or lesser risk.
